# Landing Page Brief — Draftly

## Product

**Name:** Draftly
**Tagline:** Draft on-brand copy 10x faster — without the blank page.
**One-sentence description:** An AI writing assistant for marketing teams that generates on-brand copy drafts in seconds, so your team spends time refining great work instead of starting from scratch.

**Target audience:** B2B SaaS marketing teams at companies with 10–200 employees. They're producing high volumes of copy — ads, emails, landing pages, social posts — and they're bottlenecked by the writing process, not the strategy.

**Tone:** Modern, trustworthy, and slightly playful. Think: confident senior marketer who genuinely enjoys their work. Not corporate. Not hype-y. Direct and warm.

---

## Brand direction

**Primary colors:**
- Background: deep navy `#0f1117` (dark-mode base)
- Primary accent: electric violet `#7c5cfc` (CTAs, highlights)
- Secondary accent: soft coral `#f77c5f` (badges, callouts)
- Body text: off-white `#e8e9ed`
- Muted text: slate `#8b8fa8`

**Typography direction:** Clean, modern sans-serif for headings (Inter or similar). Slightly warmer serif or medium-weight sans for body. Generous line-height.

**Visual feel:** Clean dark-mode SaaS. Cards with subtle borders and very light background lifts. Minimal but not cold. Small, tasteful animations (gradient, hover states) — nothing that distracts from the message.

### 2. Features grid (3 cards)

**Feature 1 — On-Brand from Day One**
Your brand voice, tone guidelines, and approved phrases — loaded once, applied everywhere. Every draft sounds like your team wrote it.

**Feature 2 — Draft in Seconds, Not Hours**
Paste in a brief, pick a format (email, ad, social, landing page), and get three complete drafts in under ten seconds. Pick one, refine, ship.

**Feature 3 — Team Collaboration Built In**
Comment, approve, and version drafts in one place. No more emailing Google Docs back and forth. The whole team stays in sync.

## Output spec

- Single self-contained HTML/CSS/JS file
- Dark mode by default (light mode toggle optional but not required)
- Fully responsive (mobile-first, breakpoints at 640px and 1024px)
- No external CDN dependencies
- Animations: subtle hero background gradient, hover states on cards and buttons
- Accessible: WCAG AA contrast, focus states on all interactive elements
