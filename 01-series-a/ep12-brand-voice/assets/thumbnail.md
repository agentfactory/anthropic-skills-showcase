# Thumbnail concept — Ep 12 brand-voice

**Dimensions:** 1280x720, <2 MB, JPG/PNG
**Style anchor:** Dark-mode brand palette, high-contrast headline, structured document artifact visible on screen.

## Elements

- **Background:** `#141413` (Anthropic dark). No gradients, no texture — flat dark.
- **Main headline:** "FIND YOUR BRAND VOICE IN 5 MINUTES" — Poppins Black, 108pt, color `#faf9f5`, tight leading. Line breaks after "VOICE" for rhythm.
- **Underline accent:** 8px orange bar (`#d97757`), 260px wide, immediately under the headline block.
- **Episode tag:** "EP 12 · brand-voice" — Poppins Medium, 38pt, color `#d97757`, 16px below the orange underline.
- **Document card (right column):** Rounded rectangle, background `#1e1d1b`, border `#d97757` at 1.5px, slight drop shadow. Contains a snippet of the generated brand voice guide in Lora 22pt — 4 lines visible: "Personality: Bold but not loud", "Tone: dry wit", "DO: quietly", "DON'T: leverage". Text in `#faf9f5` with DO items accented `#788c5d` (green) and DON'T items accented `#d97757` (orange).
- **Radial glow:** `#d97757` at 12% opacity, centered behind the document card. Radius ~240px.
- **No presenter photo** — the document artifact is the hero. This mirrors the "skill does the work" narrative.

## What to avoid

- No red X marks or warning icons.
- No shocked-face reaction imagery.
- No gradient fills on the headline text.
- No more than 3 distinct text elements in the headline zone.
- No "AI-generated by..." watermarks visible in the document snippet.
- Nothing that looks like a generic brand guidelines template from Canva.

## Production

- Build in Figma at 2x (2560x1440), export at 1x for upload.
- Alternate: render in Remotion at 1280x720 and export a still frame — reuse brand constants from `remotion/src/brand.ts` for color consistency.
- The document card can be a real screenshot of the skill's output, color-graded to match the dark palette.
