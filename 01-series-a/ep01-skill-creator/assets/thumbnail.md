# Thumbnail concept — Ep 01 skill-creator

**Dimensions:** 1280×720, <2 MB, JPG/PNG
**Style anchor:** Dark-mode brand palette, high-contrast headline, one face, one artifact.

## Elements
- **Background:** `#141413` (Anthropic dark). Faint orange `#d97757` radial glow behind the presenter at 15–20% opacity.
- **Main headline:** "BUILD YOUR OWN CLAUDE SKILL IN 10 MINUTES" — Poppins Black, 120 pt, color `#faf9f5`, tight leading.
- **Underline accent:** 8 px orange bar, 280 px wide, under headline.
- **Episode tag:** "EP 01 · skill-creator" — Poppins Medium, 42 pt, color `#d97757`, below underline.
- **Presenter:** Cut-out portrait, 3/4 angle, looking toward headline. Warm key light, subtle orange rim.
- **Corner badge (optional):** Small orange pill in top-right corner — "NEW SERIES" in light text, 28 pt Poppins SemiBold.

## What to avoid
- No red arrows.
- No shocked face.
- No more than 3 text elements total.
- Nothing that looks generic-AI (gradients, neon, "future of AI" clichés).

## Production
- Build in Figma at 2× (2560×1440), export at 1× for upload.
- Alternate: render it in Remotion at composition size 1280×720 and export a still frame — reuse brand constants for color consistency.
