## Use case

Analyze three competitors in the project management space — Asana, Monday.com, and Linear — on behalf of a fictional B2B startup called **Flowdesk** (async project management for remote teams, $29/seat/mo). Output: a structured competitive brief with positioning gaps, pricing comparison, feature whitespace, ICP overlap analysis, and a battle card for the sales team.

## Target audience

Sales reps, product managers, marketing teams, and founders doing market research — anyone who needs to understand the competitive landscape quickly and turn it into something actionable.
