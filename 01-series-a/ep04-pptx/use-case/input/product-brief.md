## What we do

Stackline is an AI-powered sales pipeline tool for B2B SaaS companies. We sit between your CRM and your reps and do the work that currently falls through the cracks: identifying which deals are at risk before the rep knows, drafting the exact follow-up email for each deal stage, and surfacing the one action per deal that moves it forward.

Most pipeline tools give you visibility. Stackline gives you motion.

---

## The problem

Sales managers at Series A–C SaaS companies lose, on average, 22% of pipeline to deals that stall between stages. Not because the deals are dead — because no one sent the right message at the right time. CRMs capture what happened. They don't tell you what to do next.

The current workaround is human: a manager reviews the pipeline every Monday, flags the stuck deals, coaches the rep, and hopes the email gets sent. It's slow, it doesn't scale, and the best managers are spending 40% of their time on it.

---

## Our solution

Stackline connects to Salesforce or HubSpot via OAuth in under five minutes. It reads every deal in your pipeline — stage, last activity, contact history, and deal size — and runs a risk model on each one. Deals at risk surface automatically in a daily digest.

For each at-risk deal, Stackline drafts the follow-up: the right message for the right stage, personalized to the contact's last interaction, ready to send with one click. Reps spend less time deciding what to do and more time doing it.

- **Growth** — $149/seat/month (unlimited reps, AI drafts, manager analytics)
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing (SSO, custom models, dedicated CSM)

80% of current revenue is on Growth tier. No customer has ever downgraded.
