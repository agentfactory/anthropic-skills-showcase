# Brand Brief — Canopy

**Brand name:** Canopy

**Logo description:** An abstract tree formed by upward-pointing arrows — the arrows suggest growth and direction, while together they read as a tree canopy. Mark is dark green on a white background.

**Brand adjectives:** Purposeful. Optimistic. Grounded.

**Target audience:** B2B — sustainability officers and environmental leads at mid-market companies (500–5,000 employees) who are responsible for corporate climate commitments and ESG reporting.

**Brand feel:** Like a trusted expert who also happens to care deeply about the planet.
