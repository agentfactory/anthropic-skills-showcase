# Loopline — Business Brief

Loopline is a B2B SaaS platform that automates project workflow management for mid-market companies (100-1,000 employees). The product integrates with tools like Slack, Jira, and Notion to eliminate manual status updates, routing bottlenecks, and cross-team handoff delays. Pricing is $49 per seat per month on an annual contract, with a minimum commitment of 10 seats. Our target buyer is the VP of Operations or COO at companies in professional services, marketing agencies, and software development. We currently sell direct through an inside sales team with a 45-day average sales cycle. Historical ARR: $180k at end of Year 1, $620k at end of Year 2, $1.4M at end of Year 3. We have approximately 95 paying customers, an average contract value of $14,700 annually, and our net revenue retention is running around 112% due to seat expansion. Our current gross margin on the software is approximately 76%.
