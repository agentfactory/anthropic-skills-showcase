## Use Case Summary
Transform a raw quarterly sales CSV into a fully interactive HTML dashboard — filterable by rep, region, and product line, with live charts, KPI cards, and trend lines — no BI tool required.

**Input:** `use-case/input/sales-data.csv` — 30-row Q1-Q2 2025 sales dataset for fictional company "Vantage Analytics"
**Output:** Single-file interactive HTML dashboard with Chart.js visualizations, dropdown filters, and KPI summary cards

**Target Audience:** Data analysts, business analysts, sales ops, and anyone who needs visual reporting without a BI tool subscription.
