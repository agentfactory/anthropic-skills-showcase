# Thumbnail Concept — Episode C02

## Primary Concept: "Before / After — Phone Notes to Excel"

**Layout:** Split-screen with hard diagonal divide

**Left side (Before):**
- Phone Notes app screenshot, dark mode
- Raw job log visible: "Apr 3 - henderson backyard - mulch 8yds $180 mat + $320 labor - half paid $250 owed"
- Slightly desaturated, slightly gritty
- Small timestamp label: "2:47 PM (between jobs)"

**Right side (After):**
- Excel workbook open — Sheet 1 jobs table clearly visible
- "GreenPro Landscaping" header
- Margin % column visible with green cells (71.9%)
- Three orange OUTSTANDING rows visible
- A bar chart partially visible in the corner
- Clean, crisp, professional

**Center divider:**
- Orange right-pointing arrow (→)
- Or: lightning bolt in Anthropic orange

**Text overlay (bottom third):**
- Large: **"Know If April Was a Good Month"**
- Smaller, code font: "anthropics/xlsx"

**Color treatment:**
- Anthropic dark (#141413) background behind split
- Orange (#d97757) accent on divider and text
- Left panel muted/cool tone, right panel bright/warm

## Recommended Concept: Primary (Split-screen)

Cleaner and more YouTube-optimized. The before/after transformation reads instantly
at thumbnail scale. The phone notes vs. professional workbook contrast is immediately
legible even at 320×180.

---

## Technical Specs

- Size: 1280 × 720px (YouTube thumbnail standard)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Font: Poppins Bold for overlay text
- Primary text minimum: 60pt at 1280px width
- Both panels must be legible at thumbnail scale — test at 320×180
