# Thumbnail Concept — Episode C01

## Primary Concept: "Before / After on a Job Site"

**Layout:** Split-screen with a hard diagonal divide

**Left side (Before):**
- Phone Notes app screenshot, dark mode
- Raw inspection shorthand visible: "28sq, 3-tab T/O, GAF HDZ charcoal, $8200 flat, 40% dep"
- Slightly desaturated, gritty feel
- Small label: "2:05 PM"

**Right side (After):**
- Professional Word document — "Ridge Pro Roofing LLC" header clearly visible
- Materials table and bold $8,200 total visible below
- Clean, crisp, white background
- Small label: "2:18 PM"

**Center divider:**
- Orange arrow pointing right (→)
- Or: a lightning bolt in Anthropic orange

**Text overlay (bottom third):**
- Large: **"Quote Out the Door in 4 Minutes"**
- Smaller: "While your competitor is still typing"

**Color treatment:**
- Anthropic dark (#141413) background behind the split
- Orange (#d97757) accent on the divider and text
- Left panel slightly muted/cool, right panel bright/warm

## Alternate Concept: "The Truck Dashboard"

**Scene:** Wide shot of a contractor's truck cab, late afternoon light
- Phone on the dash showing inspection notes
- Laptop open on the passenger seat showing the finished Word quote
- Work gloves on the seat, tool belt visible
- Background: residential neighborhood

**Text:**
- Top: **"anthropics/docx"** in code font, orange

---

## Recommended Concept: Primary (Split-screen)

Cleaner, more YouTube-optimized, shows the transformation immediately.
Contrast between the rough phone notes and the polished document reads instantly
even at thumbnail scale (320x180).

---

## Technical Specs

- Size: 1280 x 720px (YouTube thumbnail standard)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Font: Poppins Bold for overlay text
- Primary text size: minimum 60pt at 1280px width
