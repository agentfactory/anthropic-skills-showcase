# Thumbnail Concept — Episode B04: pptx

**Episode:** B04 — pptx — Case Study to Sales Presentation
**Series:** B — Documents & Files

---

## Hook Text

**Primary headline:** "Case Study -> Sales Deck"  
**Secondary text:** "8 slides. Chart included. Dark theme in 15 sec."  
**Badge/label:** "anthropics/pptx"

**Rationale:** The headline captures the transformation immediately. The secondary text delivers the three specific things that make this episode worth watching: the 8-slide complete structure, the chart (which suggests real data visualization, not just text slides), and the 15-second dark theme — the unexpected speed is the hook for the iteration story.

---

## Visual Concept

The thumbnail is anchored by a strong before/after composition. The left side shows raw plain text on a dark editor background. The right side shows a clean slide — the results slide with the bar chart visible at thumbnail scale.

**Left half (before):**
A dark terminal/editor window with plain text visible. Small sections of text are highlighted, suggesting structure but not design. A label beneath reads "Plain Text Case Study" in small gray type. The overall feel is data waiting to be shaped.

**Right half (after):**
A single PowerPoint slide rendered in the clean light theme: white background, navy headline, and a visible grouped bar chart with before/after bars. The chart does not need to be legible at thumbnail size — the shape of a chart is enough. Below the slide, a second smaller dark-theme slide version is partially visible, as if stacked behind, suggesting the theme iteration story.

**Center element:**
A large arrow in Anthropic orange (#d97757) pointing from left to right. The skill badge "anthropics/pptx" sits below the arrow in the Anthropic blue (#6a9bcc) pill.

**Top-right accent:**
A small "DARK THEME" badge in white text on a deep navy (#0f172a) background, with a thin teal border (#2dd4bf). This calls out the iteration story before the viewer reads the secondary text.

**Bottom strip:**
Dark bar (#141413) with "B04" in orange and "Documents & Files" in light text.

## Thumbnail Production Notes

- Render at 1280x720 with 2x assets at 2560x1440
- The bar chart on the right half should show two bars (before/after) for at least one metric — enough visual cue to read as "data visualization" even at small size
- The "DARK THEME" badge in the top right is a secondary visual hook — it should be noticeable but not compete with the main headline
- The stacked dark slide behind the light slide on the right should be visible at about 30% opacity — present but not distracting
- Test legibility of the chart at 320x180 (mobile YouTube thumbnail size) — the bar shapes should be distinguishable at that size even if the labels are not
- A/B test against a version where the headline is "400 Words -> 8-Slide Deck" to see if the specificity of the word count increases CTR
